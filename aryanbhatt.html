GET IN TOUCH

With over a decade of leadership in the wholesale and distribution space, I’ve been honored with Top 40 Under 40, Top Business Professional of the Year, and Woman on the Rise awards. I have also trained and competed at the highest level of Ironman triathlon. I bring boundless energy, expertise in communications, marketing, and operations, and a focus on long-term success to the businesses I acquire. I believe those running the business know it best, and I am here to steward their legacy and enhance their vision. My aim is not to buy and sell, but to sustain and grow.


My Search

I am looking for a profitable, well-established, high quality distribution company with sustainable competitive advantage, steady cash flows, growth potential, strong leadership with EBITDA between $2-5 million.

My Impact

I believe that fun is an essential ingredient for excellence. People are always at the core of everything I do. I follow through on my commitments and view a company as a living, breathing entity. I adapt quickly to emerging changes, fostering continuous improvement and resilience.
